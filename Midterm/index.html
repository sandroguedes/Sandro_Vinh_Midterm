Midterm`s Cats vs Dogs

Dogs are awesome / Cats are lame

By Sandro Guedes

Dogs are humanity`s oldest animal buddies, and it shows! After about 15.000 years walking alongside us, they`ve evolved into the perfect pets for people of all ages and dispositions.

Throughout all those years, they have made crude attempts at pleasing us humans into a form of art. Dogs are, in fact, so good at reading human behaviour that they can actually predict our dispotions to a degree, all in order to please us, their "hoomans".

Cats, on the other hand, are nothing but wingless buzzards. They couldn't care less about us, as long as we are both feeding them and cleaning their litter boxes.

PS - This last bit is a big fat lie, as I own both a dog a two cats lol
